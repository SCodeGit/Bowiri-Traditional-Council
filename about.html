About Bowiri Traditional Council

Our Heritage

The Bowiri Traditional Council traces its lineage across generations, uniting history, culture, and leadership. The council preserves customs, settles disputes, and fosters development within the community.

Our Mission

To uphold the values of peace, unity, and progress while protecting the cultural and spiritual heritage of the Bowiri people.

Territory

Bowiri encompasses several towns and villages rich in history and tradition. The council remains dedicated to the growth and wellbeing of these communities.
