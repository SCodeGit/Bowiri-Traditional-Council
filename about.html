About Bowiri

Who We Are

The Bowiri Traditional Council stands as the custodian of the customs, lineage, and ancestral heritage of the Bowiri people. Across generations, the Council has played a major role in governance, dispute resolution, cultural preservation, and community development.

Our Mission

To promote unity, safeguard cultural values, and support the social and economic welfare of all Bowiri communities.

Territory

The Bowiri jurisdiction comprises several towns and villages under the authority and custodianship of the Council. Community maps and detailed descriptions may be provided on request.
