Community Projects & Support

Empowering Bowiri through sustainable development, education, and cultural advancement.

1. Educational Support Fund

The Council offers bursaries and scholarships to brilliant but needy students from Bowiri communities pursuing secondary and tertiary education.

This initiative has supported over 80 students since 2018, with funds sourced through voluntary donations, cultural events, and partnerships with NGOs. The program also encourages beneficiaries to return and contribute to local development after graduation.

2. Community Health Outreach

Periodic health screening and vaccination programs organized in collaboration with the Ghana Health Service.

These programs target maternal health, malaria prevention, and youth awareness on hygiene and nutrition. The Traditional Council ensures participation by mobilizing community volunteers and local leaders.

3. Cultural Heritage Restoration

Renovation of royal buildings and preservation of traditional artifacts for future generations.

Through support from diaspora groups and local artisans, the Council is restoring sacred sites and documenting oral traditions to preserve Bowiri identity and values.

4. Youth Skill Development

Vocational training programs for young people in agriculture, ICT, and small business management.

Each year, over 50 youth participants receive hands-on training and mentorship. The project aims to reduce unemployment and migration by building self-reliance among the youth.
