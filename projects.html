Ongoing & Completed Projects

Every initiative strengthens our heritage and uplifts the Bowiri people. Below are some of our key community efforts:

Education Support Program

Providing scholarships, teaching materials, and infrastructure support for schools within the Bowiri Traditional Area.

Community Health Outreach

Regular health screenings, maternal care, and medical supply donations for underserved communities.

Cultural Heritage Preservation

Reviving traditional festivals, preserving artefacts, and promoting cultural education among the youth.

Clean Water Initiative

Drilling boreholes and rehabilitating old water systems to ensure clean, safe drinking water for all Bowiri towns.
