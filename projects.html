Community Projects & Development

Empowering the Bowiri land through sustainable progress and cultural unity.

1. Bowiri Educational Support Initiative

This project provides scholarships and learning resources to deserving students within the Bowiri enclave, ensuring that no child is left behind due to financial constraints.

2. Road Rehabilitation & Access Development

Through community-driven partnerships, major feeder roads have been rehabilitated to improve trade, mobility, and access to essential services.

3. Bowiri Cultural Heritage Center

A preservation hub for local art, history, and oral traditions. The center promotes cultural pride, tourism, and serves as a resource for youth education.
