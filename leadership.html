Community Projects & Initiatives

The Bowiri Traditional Council continues to promote development across education, health, and culture. Below are key projects currently ongoing, with opportunities for public support.

School Renovation — Bowiri Primary

Upgrading classrooms and learning materials for over 450 pupils to ensure a brighter future for Bowiri’s children.

Clean Water Initiative

Drilling boreholes and maintaining safe drinking water systems in remote communities.

Health Outreach Programme

Organizing mobile clinics and vaccination campaigns in partnership with district health services.
