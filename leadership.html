Nana Osiadieyor Kwabena Salo V

Paramount Chief (Bowirimanhene)

Nana Osiadieyor Kwabena Salo V was born on 5th October, 1999 at Bowiri Anyinase in the Bayida Clan in the Biakoye District of the Oti Region. His parent are Mr. Eric Kwabena and Mrs. Bertha Akosua Nyame, all from Bowiri Anyinase.

He start his primary education at Worawora Anglican Primary school and the JHS and completed in 2017. He was a student at Bishop Herman College at Kpando from 2017 to 2020.

He taught as a pupil-Teacher in the Stars On Earth Academy in Accra Madina for 4 years from 2020 to 2024. He is now a student of Peki College of Education offering the Bachelor's Degree in Education.

He was enstooled on Friday 13th October, 2023 at Bowiri Anyinase as the Paramount Chief of Bowiri Traditional Area(Bowirimanhene). He is an organist and enjoys soft music.

Nana Kwasi Asiedu II

Sub-Divisional Chief (Ankobehene)

Nana Kwasi Asiedu II was born on Sunday 2nd December 1973 to Inspector John Kofi Anso Bandua of blessed memory and Madam Gertrude Theodora Abena Asiedu-Bandua, both from Bowiri Anyinase. Nana started his Basic School at Kumasi Presby/Methodist primary one to six (1-6) from 1979-1985. He continued to Kumasi Methodist Day JHS 1-3 in 1986 - 1988.

He worked at KBL as a driver for three years from 1989-1991. He joined the Ministry of Defence as a Civilian driver from 1997 to date. Nana was enstooled as Chief with his stool name as Nana Kwasi Asiedu II on 19th November, 2021 as Sub-Divisinal Chief.

Private name of chief is Jacob Kwasi Anso Bandaua as ANKOBEAHENE OF BOWIRI TRADITIONAL AREA. from BAWUNYA CLAN of Bowiri Anyinase.

Nana Yaa Sikebea II

Kyidomhemaa (Divisional Queenmother)

Nana Yaa Sikebea II was born on 9th April, 1964 at Bowiri Kwamekrom to Mr. Kofi Kumah Bediako and Madam Afua Atukpawu all of blessed memory.

She started her Primary School Education at Bowiri Kwamekrom E.P Primary in 1970.

She was taken to Bowiri Amanfrom to continue Primary five in 1974. At Primary six in 1975 she lost her father. Being left with nobody to care for school education, she returned to Bowiri Kwamekrom and started learning to trade and farm.

She was enstooled on 5th August, 2022 on the title of Kyidomhemaa at Bowiri Kwamekrom with the status of a Divisional Queenmother

Nana Kwasi Okyerefor III

Tufuhene

Nana Kwasi Okyerefor III was born on 11th March, 1969 at Bowiri Aboabo. He started Basic School in 1976, continued at Bowiri Takrabe L/A Middle School. Obtained Middle School Leaving Certificate in 1986. Gained employment with Abibigroma Theater Company, School of Performing Arts, Legon. In 1993 worked with National Theater, Accra for four(4) additional years a total of eleven(11) years working experience with Abibigroma Theater Company.

He travelled to USA for greener pastures in 1997. In USA, worked with Gate Gourmet between 1998 and 2001. He also worked as a shuttle bus driver for preflight packing company in USA from 2001-2003. I persuade a diploma certificate in Transport Management. After acquisition of the diploma Certificate, I worked with American Transportation USA 2003- 2005, JB. Hunt Transportation USA 2005-2008, Knight Transportation USA 2008-2016.

In 2016, I registered my own company as Efo Ike Logistics Inc USA as C.E.O till date. I was enstooled at home on the 13th August,2021 as Nana Kwasi Okyerefor III the Tufuhene of Bowiri Traditional Area at Bowiri Aboabo.

Nana Yaa Asiedua I

Queenmother

Nana Yaa Asiedua I was born on Thursday 3rd September, 1981 at Bowiri Anyinase to Mr. Solomon G.Y. Asiedu of Bowiri Anyinase and Mrs. Mary Abena Asiedu of Bodada-Buem.

She started her Basic Education at Sogakofe L/A primary school from 1985 to 1991and 2 years at Sogakofe L/A JHS from 1991 to 1994. She did one year at Jasikan College of Education Demonstration JHS in 1995/1996 and entered Ho Mawuli Senior High School in 1996/1997 and completed in 1999 with school Cert: O" Level. She was a celebrated Athlete in both Track and Field events which earned her great Laurels. She entered Tamale Sawaba Secretariate school in order to update herself in Computer Literacy and completed in 2003.

She is married and has four children, two boys and two girls. She is Self- employed. Her hobbies are: Listening to soft music and Films.

Nana Kwasi Sike II

Kyidomhene

Nana Kwasi Sike II was born on 25th December 1984 at Bowiri Kwamekrom to Cephas Kudzo Yerenkyi-Bekoe and Mawuse Semanhia both from Bowiri Kwamekrom.He started E.P. Basic Education in 1986 and 1990 -1992 at Akpafu Odomi continued at Hohoe E.P. primary 4 and completed Hohoe JHS in 1998. He attended Alavanyo E.P. Trade Training Center learnt Block laying, 2000 to 2002 studied at Purity Technology Institute Ho -2005 for HND , studied Building Technology at HO Technical University and completed in 2009.

At KNUST Obtained BSC in Quantity Surveying and Construction Economics completed in June 2015 . 2024 once again offered admission into KNUST to Peruse MSC in construction Management. In 2011 offered a job at Fathers Dogli Memorial Institute at New Ayoma 2011 - 2013 , posted to Akatsi South Municipal Assembly as Technical Engineer in Works Dept. 2018-2020. Ga East Municipal Assembly as Senior Technical Engineer in Works Dept then Ledzokuku Municipal Assembly at Assist Quantity Surveyor, August 2020 till date.

He was enstooled on Friday 5th August,2022 as the chief of Bowiri Kwamekrom, Title of office as Kyidomhene of Bowiri Traditional Area Married and has 3 children. He loves singing and makes friends that impact life positively.

Nana Kwasi Asantey II

Gyasehene

Nana Kwasi Asantey II with my private name as Obiadie Kwasi Foster was born in the year 1976 to Mr. Odeifor Kwasi John and Madam Afua Victoria Oheneasah all from Bowiri Amanfrom.

I started my primary school education at Bowiri Kwamekrom in the year 1983 and continued at Bowiri Amanfrom E.P Junior High School and completed in the year 1992.

I went through the carpentry course for three years and now a full time carpenter and at the same time a businessman.

I am with a wife and seven(7) children. I was enstooled a chief on the 18th November, 2022 the Gyasehene for Bowiri Kwamekrom with the stool name as Nana Kwasi Asantey II.
